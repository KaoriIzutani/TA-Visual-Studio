Tidbits on Ginger Cats

Known for years as either “marmalade cats” or “ginger cats", the orange tabby has been nicknamed to be distinct from the black tabby variety. It’s no wonder that so many orange tabbies end up with names like Ginger, Big Red, Marmalade, or even Tangerine.

"Of all of God's creatures, there is only one that cannot be made the slave of the lash. That one is a cat." --Mark Twain

Orange cats tend to have a laid-back “cattitude” and can make them excellent lap buddies and snugglers. On the other hand, it may make them more at risk for becoming overweight and obese.
